# Quantifiable, Objective Skill Management in Wipro through NFTs

---

## Problem Statement

Current skill management at Wipro faces two core challenges:

- **Manual & Subjective**: Skill assessment relies on resume matching, lacking objectivity.
- **Intangible Skill Transfers**: When an employee moves between eHubs/GBLs for a specific skill, all their other skills move with them — and this collateral skill loss **cannot be tracked**.

---

## Proposed Solution

An **NFT-based Skill Management System** that quantifies skills at both the **organizational** and **individual** level.

- Each individual accumulates **NFTs per skill**.
- The organization estimates its skill requirements in terms of **NFT quantities** for each skill.

### 1. 📊 Organizational Skill Estimation
> Wipro needs **X NFTs** of Angular skill; currently holds **Y NFTs**; gap of **X–Y NFTs** to be accumulated.

### 2. 🔁 Cross-Unit Skill Lending
> Loan **X NFTs** of Angular from **Americas-1** to **Europe**.

### 3. 🤖 Analytics & AI Integration
> Apply analytics and predictive/preventive AI models on top of NFT data for workforce planning.

## Forgetting NFT (Forgetful NFT)

> *An innovation for the education & skill sector.*

A skill's value **diminishes over time** unless actively refreshed — mirroring established research in cognitive science and business economics (diminishing returns).

### How It Works

- Accumulated NFTs **continuously lose value** if the skill is not exercised.
- Value **stagnates at a lower threshold** if left unrefreshed.
- Value is **restored** when the individual engages with the skill through:
  - 🏗️ Projects
  - 📚 Trainings

### Foundation
Based on research from **medical and psychological fields** on the rate of human knowledge decay, as well as business concepts around **diminishing returns**.
