# Employee Journey – Domain Requirements

## Business Context

- ABC Company has been using **Legacy software** for Employee Management right
  from Employee onboarding – offboarding activities, Employee PII data, Salary
  data management, Employee engagement data, Employee competency
  management and so on.
- The current legacy system has **limitations to scale** to support future
  expansions of the company and has multiple integrations to various other
  software for different purposes, few of which are in-house and few 3rd-Party
  products.
- Some of these integrated applications are also **legacy products** and do not
  support latest integration platforms.
- The company is foreseeing **growth at the rate of 10% year on year**, which
  translates to increasing their employee base — calling for an Employee
  Management platform that supports this requirement and ensures optimal
  performance.

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

- Application should seamlessly support **increased employee base** as the company grows.
- Enhanced employee management, streamlined HR processes, improved talent
  management, insights for Projects and HR Management, Employee recognition and rewards.
- As the company is **HQ in Europe**, necessary **GDPR norms** have to be adhered
  to in the solution design.
- Design should encompass **user-friendly interface**, personalization and
  customization options, and employee self-service options.
- The application would be accessed by employees on **Desktops / Laptops / Mobile / Tabs**
  and responsive design principles have to be followed.
- **Analytics and reporting** with reports catering to various stakeholders within
  the organization.
- **Role-based access control (RBAC)** mechanisms to be employed.

---

## Solution Expectations

1. Clearly identify all the **functional and non-functional requirements**, treat the
   requirements as per the **SMART framework** and restate properly.

2. Present your understanding of the **current and future-state architectures**,
   what architectural principles would you apply, and what will be your
   architectural pattern best fit for the given case.

3. Discuss in detail on your **data analytics and data storage design strategies**
   to support the scalability and reporting requirements highlighted.

4. Elaborate on the **Integration approach**.

5. Present a view on the **data migration approach** without impacting day-to-day
   activities, which can't have any downtime impacting daily activities of
   employees in the company.

6. Consider **multiple solution options** and present one **final solution in detail**
   with implementation plan and effort estimations where appropriate.
